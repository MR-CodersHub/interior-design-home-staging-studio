Investment Clarifications

Furniture leasing fees are scaled based on the campaign duration (usually 1-3 months) and the size/value of the property. Standard contracts cover logistics, delivery, styling setup, and removal.

Bespoke design is structures into 3 main milestones: (1) 40% initial conceptualization deposit, (2) 40% material procurement phase, and (3) 20% turnkey hand-off upon custom styling installation.
