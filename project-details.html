THE DESIGN BRIEF

Cinematic Depth.
Textural Harmony.

Crafted as an architectural haven, this Bel Air modern estate represents our design ethos in its absolute purist form.

We began by stripping away cold white partitions and replacing them with structural sandblasted concrete boundaries. We layered custom deep emerald velvet sofas to anchor the central conversation pit, contrasted with heavy raw charcoal slate tiling.

By integrating a sophisticated warm LED network, we cast soft visual glows into specific corners. The result is a home that transforms dramatically at night, mimicking an intimate boutique hotel environment.
